Carbonara Recipe

  • Time: 35 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Price
Bacon $6.50
Water $0
Garlic $5.50
Spaghetti $3.30
Parmesean $5
Eggs $4
Salt and Pepper $5
Parsley $2

Step 1: Add bacon and water to a skillet and bring to a simmer.

Step 2:Continue simmering until water is evaporated, then continue to cook the bacon until crispy.

Step 3: Remove bacon from pan and reserve the drippings.

Step 4: Saute garlic in that same skillet until golden brown, then add to a bowl with 1 tablespoon bacon fat, eggs, egg yolk, Parmesan and pepper. Mix well.

Step 5: Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti or linguine pasta until al dente. Once cooked, drain pasta and reserve 1 cup of the cooking water.

Step 6: Slowly pour the hot cooking water into the egg mixture. Then pour over the hot pasta and toss to coat. Add crumbled bacon.

Step 7: Let pasta rest for a few minutes, tossing frequently until the carbonara sauce thickens. Serve immediately with a sprinkle of fresh parsley.

Penne Alla Vodka Recipe

  • Time: 35 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Price
Butter $2
Tomato Paste $5.25
Red Pepper Flakes $4.50
Penne $3.30
Parmesean $5
Cream $4
Salt and Pepper $5
Parsley $2

Step 1: Start by gently cooking the shallot and garlic in butter, stirring often with a wooden spoon (better for scraping the pan later on), until softened and translucent but not browned.

Step 2:In go the tomato paste and red pepper flakes. Cook, stirring often with a wooden spoon, until the paste coats the alliums and is starting to darken.

Step 3: Now stir in the vodka, scraping up any browned bits, and remove the pot from the heat; we’ll get back to it in a minute!

Step 4: Use a large pot and be sure to salt the water generously.

Step 5: Mhen the pasta is al dente (meaning it’s still just a wee bit firm at the core), scoop out 2 cups of the pasta water and then drain the pasta.

Step 6: Sprinkle half of the Parmesan over and stir until melted and combined.

Step 7: Now turn off the heat and stir in the drained pasta and remaining Parmesan. If the pasta is looking a little dry, stir in more pasta water, a tablespoon at a time.

Step 8: Now divide into portions and serve.

Pesto Pasta Recipe

  • Time: 35 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Price
Pesto Sauce $6.50
Pasta of choice ~$3.25
Parmesean $5
Salt and Pepper $5
Parsley $2

Step 1: Bring a large pot of water to the boil with the salt.

Step 2:Add pasta and cook for the length of time per the packet.

Step 3: Just before draining, scoop out 1 cup of of the pasta cooking water.

Step 4: Drain pasta in a colander, leave it for a minute.

Step 5: Transfer pasta to a bowl (do not use pasta cooking pot, too hot).

Step 6: Add pesto and 1/4 cup of pasta water. Toss to coat pasta in pesto, adding more water if required to make pasta silky and saucy, rather than dry and sticky.

Step 7: Taste, add more salt and pepper if desired.

Step 8: Serve immediately, garnished with fresh parmesan.
